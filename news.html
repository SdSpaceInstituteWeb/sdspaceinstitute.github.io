March 15, 2026

SDSI Recognized by IRS as 501(c)(3) Public Charity

The IRS formally recognized SDSI as a tax-exempt public charity under Section 501(c)(3), effective April 10, 2025. The determination confirms SDSI's eligibility to receive tax-deductible contributions and supports continued expansion of programs in applied aerospace research, technology development, and mission operations and research development.

October 14, 2025

NASA California Space Grant Selection

SDSI, SJSU, and SDSU were selected for two awards under NASA's 2025 Workforce Development Program to support Sentinel-Bio+ development.

November 20, 2025

SDSI Attends UC San Diego Space Day Forum

SDSI President Dr. Sean Casey and Board Director Dave Strobel attended UC San Diego's Space Day Forum, engaging with representatives from DARPA, SpaceWERX, Viasat, and UC San Diego research teams.

December 24, 2025

Sentinel-Bio+ Internal Design Review Complete

SDSI completed an internal Preliminary Design Review for Sentinel-Bio+. No blocking deficiencies were identified. The program cleared to proceed toward an external PDR planned for early 2026.
